About

Simple, private, in-browser tools. Made for humans, not by marketing bots.

What this is

Utility Tools by The Apollonian Eye is a small arsenal of web utilities that run entirely in your browser: text transforms, image compression/formatting, audio/PDF tweaks.

Why it exists

Because most “free tools” sites are slow, noisy, or invasive. We prefer fast pages, clean UI, and files that never leave your device.
